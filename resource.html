Mastering your technical interview:

During your interview, you will be asked questions that center around these common data structures and algorithms:

~Arrays

~Hash Tables

~Strings

~Linked Lists

~Matrix

~Stack

~Two Pointer

~Binary Search Tree

~Breadth First Search

~Depth First Search


Looking at these for the first time can seem intimidating! However, with a solid study schedule you will be able to master these topics. There are many tutorials on Youtube that have in-depth explanations as to how each othe the data structures and algorithms work. Resources like Leetcode and Hackerank are also the best tools you can use during your technical preparation as they ask interview style technical questions and even have commonly asked questions on their website.
